Document Title: HIPAA Privacy and Security Compliance Policy
Effective Date: January 1, 2025
Version: 1.0

1. Purpose
This policy ensures the confidentiality, integrity, and availability of Protected Health Information (PHI) in accordance with the Health Insurance Portability and Accountability Act (HIPAA) of 1996.

2. Scope
Applies to all employees, contractors, and vendors who create, access, or transmit PHI.

3. Policy

Access Controls: PHI is accessible only to authorized personnel.

Data Encryption: PHI must be encrypted during storage and transmission.

Training: Employees must complete annual HIPAA training.

Incident Reporting: Any breach of PHI must be reported within 24 hours.

Auditing: Regular audits will verify HIPAA compliance.

Business Associate Agreements (BAAs): Required for third-party partners handling PHI.

4. Enforcement
Violations of this policy may result in disciplinary action, including termination.
