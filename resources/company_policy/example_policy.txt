Document Title: Health Data Security and Privacy Policy
Effective Date: February 1, 2025
Version: 2.1

1. Purpose
This policy defines how the company protects health-related data and ensures compliance with federal and industry regulations.

2. Scope
Applies to all employees, contractors, vendors, and systems that store or process health-related data.

3. Data Protection Measures

All electronic records must be stored on encrypted servers.

Transmission of sensitive data must use secure communication channels (TLS 1.2+).

Access to sensitive data requires unique user credentials and MFA.

Activity logs must be maintained for all data access attempts.

Incident reports must be filed within 48 hours of discovery.

Annual security training is mandatory for all staff.

Business partners must sign a data handling agreement before accessing systems.

4. Review and Maintenance
This policy will be reviewed annually or when regulatory changes occur.
